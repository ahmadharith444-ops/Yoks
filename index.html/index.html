Owl Species

Barn Owl (Tyto alba)

The Barn Owl is widely used in Malaysian plantations as a natural rodent controller. It is easily recognised by its pale, heart-shaped face and silent flight.

Conservation in Malaysia

Owls play an important role in Malaysian ecosystems by controlling pest populations naturally. Habitat loss, deforestation, and monoculture farming threaten many owl species.

Protecting forests, maintaining large trees, and using nest boxes in agricultural areas can help ensure owls continue to thrive alongside human development.
